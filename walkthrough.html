🔍 Phase 1: The Multi-Timeframe (MTF) Brain

The core of v4.0's accuracy is its two-stage filtering process:

1. Daily Trend Filter (Long-Term)

Before any technical indicators are checked, the engine analyzes the Daily Timeframe.

  • Metric: Simple Moving Average (SMA) 100.
  • Logic: The stock must be trading above its Daily SMA 100.
  • Purpose: This ensures we only trade stocks in a structural uptrend, preventing "catching a falling knife."
2. Hourly Execution (Immediate Momentum)

If the daily trend is bullish, the engine switches to the 1-Hour Timeframe:

  • RSI Check: Verifies if RSI is ≤ 35 (Oversold condition).
  • MACD Confirmation: Looks for a Bullish Crossover (MACD line crossing above Signal line) within the last 3 candles.
  • VWAP Alignment: Confirms if the price is Above VWAP to ensure intraday buyer dominance.

⚡ Phase 2: High Conviction Filtering

A signal is upgraded to 🔥 HIGH CONVICTION if it meets the technical criteria AND features:

  • Volume Spike: Volume is > 1.5x higher than the 20-day average.
  • VWAP Confirmation: Price is definitively trading above the session's Volume Weighted Average Price.

📲 Phase 3: Real-Time Synchronization

Once a signal is detected, v4.0 executes a triple-sync operation:

  • 1. Telegram Alert: Sends a structured message with an AI Analysis Note explaining the setup to your subscribers.
  • 2. Google Sheets Sync: Appends a new row to your "US STOCK AI" spreadsheet using the GCP_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_KEY for secure, mobile-friendly tracking.
  • 3. CSV Repository Update: Overwrites active_signals.csv and market_overview_technical.csv in your GitHub repository to feed the dashboard.

🖥️ Phase 4: The Quant Dashboard (Streamlit)

The final layer is the visual terminal:

  • RSI Heatmap: Visualizes the entire market universe by RSI levels and Market Cap.
  • Live Signal Feed: Displays the most recent high-probability setups in a high-contrast Neon Cyan table.
  • Deep Analysis: Allows you to pull interactive candlestick charts with SMA 100 and VWAP overlays for any specific ticker.
